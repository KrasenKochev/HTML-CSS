Easy and healthy carrot muffins

These easy healthy carrot muffins are made with oatmeal, coconut and honey to give you a healthy start to your day and can be the best guilt-free snack.

Prep time: 10 minutes || Cook time: 25 minutes || Servings: 12 || Calories: 162kcal

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ½ tsp ground ginger
  • 3 bananas
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup milk of your choice
  • 2 tbsp oil of your choice (coconut, canola, avocado)
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 cups grated carrots (approximately 3 large carrots)
  • 1 cup cranberries/raisins (optional)
  • ½ cup desiccated coconut

For the cream cheese icing (optional)

  • 1 cup cream cheese (at room temperature)
  • ½ cup honey (or more to taste)
  • 1 cup toasted coconut for topping

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 180ºC/350ºF and line a 12-hole muffin pan with paper liners. Lightly spray the liners with non-stick cooking spray as the muffin batter can be sticky and stick to the liners.
  2. To make the batter, blend the oats in a strong food processor/blender until oats are the consistency of flour.
  3. Transfer to a mixing bowl and mix in the baking powder, salt and spices.
  4. In a separate bowl, mash the bananas and mix in the oil, eggs, milk, vanilla and honey.
  5. Pour into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined then fold in the carrots, raisins/cranberries and coconut.
  6. Spoon the batter into the prepared liners and place in the oven.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes until a skewer inserted comes out clean. Remove from the oven and allow to cool completely.
  8. If making the icing, beat together the honey and cream cheese until smooth. Spread the icing onto the muffins and top with toasted coconut.

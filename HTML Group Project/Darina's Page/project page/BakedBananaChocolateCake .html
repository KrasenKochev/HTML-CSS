Baked Banana Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 400g bananas (about 2 bananas)
  • 3 eggs
  • 40g coconut flour (about 3 heaped tablespoons)
  • 80g chocolate (85% cocoa)
  • 1 packet of baking powder

Instructions:

  1. Take the two bananas and bake them in the oven at 250°C for 15 minutes. This duration is enough for the bananas to turn black while the insides cook, maximizing the banana aroma.
  2. Once baked, remove the bananas from the oven, peel them, and place the soft flesh into a bowl. Mash them using a fork or a potato masher. Let it cool for about 5-10 minutes.
  3. After cooling, break the eggs into the banana mixture and mix thoroughly.
  4. Add the coconut flour and baking powder to the banana-egg mixture and stir again.
  5. Break the chocolate into small pieces and mix it into the batter.
  6. Transfer the mixture into a baking tin and bake in a preheated oven at 180°C for 30-35 minutes.

Health Benefits:

  • Bananas: A good source of potassium, dietary fiber, and antioxidants. They aid in digestion, promote heart health, and provide essential vitamins and minerals.
  • Eggs: Packed with high-quality proteins, essential amino acids, and various essential vitamins and minerals.
  • Coconut Flour: High in fiber, protein, and healthy fats. It's also free from wheat and other grains.
  • Chocolate (85% cocoa): Rich in flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties. It also contains essential minerals like iron, magnesium, and zinc.
